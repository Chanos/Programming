Mission Statement

We will research the concept of “Cloud Computing” and its applicable use in the business world. We will accomplish this objective by first defining the concept of cloud computing, and describe its characteristics. Once the concept of cloud computing is identified, we will describe its different models and how they can be deployed. We will then identify and examine any issues that may exist with cloud computing. After we have defined cloud computing, described its models, and identified its issues; we will provide examples of cloud computing’s various uses in the business world. Finally we will discuss the pros and cons of cloud computing.
