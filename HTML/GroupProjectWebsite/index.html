Welcome to Thomas M. Brady, American Legion Post 45 located at 160 McClure St Canton GA, 30144.

The post is active in most of the Legion programs. The first American Legion baseball team in Cherokee County in over 50 years was started in 2003. The post always sends a group to Ga. Boys State. The post honors law enforcement officers and fire fighters in March and does the American Legion School Awards program in April. The post is the sponsor for the Cherokee County Fair held every September for the past 77 years.

At the state convention in June 2004, Thomas M. Brady Post 45 was recognized as the Outstanding Post in Category 4 and received the Children and Youth Award in Category 3.

Membership is open to all veterans with an honorable discharge and one day of service during a war period, or service men and women who are still serving on active duty. Go to galegion.org for more information on membership.
