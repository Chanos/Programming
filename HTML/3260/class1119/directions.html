ETOWAH HIGH SCHOOL
6565 Putnam Ford Dr
Woodstock, GA 30189-1501

Turn left onto Jamerson Rd.  Turn right onto Canton Road.  Continue to follow Canton Road 2.3 miles.  Turn left onto Towne Lake Pkwy.  Travel 2.1 miles; keep left at the fork to continue on Towne Lake Pkwy. 0.1 miles.   Towne Lake Pkwy becomes Eagle Drive.  Travel 0.5 miles Turn right onto Putnam Ford Drive.  Travel 0.1 miles.  End at 6565 Putnam Ford Dr Woodstock, GA 30189-1501    Estimated Time: 13 minutes Estimated Distance: 5.55 miles.

 

HARRISON HIGH SCHOOL
4500 Due West Road
Kennesaw, GA

Begin on Jamerson Road. Turn left onto Canton Hwy. Right onto Chastain (Kroger’s on right side) Turn right onto New Chastain which becomes Chastain Road and then will turn into McCollum Pkwy. Turn right onto Highway 41 (also called Cobb Parkway) and go 3-4 miles to top of hill and turn left onto Kennesaw Due West Road (Look for Amoco Station and Mexican Restaurant at that corner). Once you are on Kennesaw Due West Road, go 6 1/2 miles straight - Harrison High School will be on your right.
